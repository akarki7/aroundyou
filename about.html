About Us

What is around you?

Around You is a platform for young students from Bremen Nord where they are able to find all the best places and services such as Food Delivery, Shopping Malls, Clubs, Travel destinations etc. around them. We do the research for our customers so they do not have to spend hours looking at different options. We have two main platforms - the main one being the website and the other being our instagram page. With the completely digital process, our customers not only save time and effort, but also find the best places and services around them. No annoying visits and no hidden costs. Just try it out for yourself!
